Fear of Missing Out

Most companies fear that if they are not actively participating in new technologies then they might miss out on big opportunities. This could lead to their competitors getting an upper hand.

AI is an algorithm used to mimic human intelegence

This has led to companies adding AI everywhere even if there isn't much demand. Nowadays, whenever people hear something has AI now, they either don't care or worse, roll their eyes over another pointless feature forced onto them.
